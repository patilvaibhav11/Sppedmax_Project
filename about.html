Our Story

If you’re here, chances are you care about the deep, underlying technologies that power the internet. Well, you’re in good company. We are a world-class Internet Service Provider (ISP) that caters to both home and business segments.

If you live in a large urban area, you may be frustrated by poor internet service on occasion. Things like buffering, lagging and slow download speeds can put a damper on things like video chatting with loved ones, binge-watching your favorite shows, and getting work done at home. So, just imagine having a lack of Internet access in rural areas would make it so harder for farmers, students, people to get their work done. By keeping customers’ needs in mind to provide unlimited, high-speed internet to every corner of the world, we have started Speedmaxx internet services.
